About us

Kalaikoil Natiyapalli – Kilasivalpatti, one of the branches of Kalaikoil dance academy – Karaikudi, is a popular centre of excellence in Bharatanatyam, started in 2016 with 10 students in the locality and currently expanded to 25 students. Out of which one of the students has presented her Arangetram in the year 2019 and few more students are in the pipeline to present their debut in coming days. The students have performed across the place for many regional festivals and dance programmes. The motive of this dance school is to spread the Art form around the rural areas and bring out talents from there. Now the online classes are also provided to make the Bharatanatyam Art accessible for the students globally.
