Guru

Kalaimamani Dr Sarala, the founder of Kalaikoil dance academy – Karaikudi, with an excellent training from her Guru Dr Padma Subrahmanyam, is celebrated as one of the most renowned classical dancer and dance scholar. With her three decades of experience in teaching Bharatanatyam, she has trained more than 1000 young aspirants and many of her students are conducting classes and spreading the art form throughout the world. For her contribution to the field of classical art, she was conferred the “Kalaimamani” (the highest civilian award in the state of Tamilnadu, India), and many other awards like “Thalaikolarivai”, “Silambisai Selvi” and “Bharatha Kalai Sudar” etc.

Arangetram

With an impeccable training for four years in Bharatanatyam, Mrs Balambika Valliappan presented her arangetram in the year 2009 at LCTL Palaniappa Chettiar Memorial Auditorium – Karaikudi, in the presence of Tamilannal Ra.Ma. Periyakaruppan, a notable Tamil scholar of the twentieth century and KR. Periyakaruppan, the current Minister of Rural development and the former minister of Hindu religious and Charitable endowments in the state of Tamilnadu, India.
