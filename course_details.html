Course details

Curriculum for each student is customised as per their learning capability, below given details are for general reference only

Beginner Level 1

approx 6 months

Excercises

Preparation to learn Adavus, It strengthen your arms, calf and feet muscles and makes your thigh muscles and hip more flexible

Body Alignment

Learning the basic half sitting position "Aramadai" and arm position "Natyarambam". Special focus will be given to the position of upper torso according to the dance movement. 
They learn how to add grace to the body inspite of maintaining linear line.

Hastas

Learning of double hand posture "Asamyuktha hastha".
Learning of feet movement "Pada bhedam" and head movements "Shiro bhedam" 
Learning of dancing position "Sthanakam"

Adavus

Adavus are the basic steps of Bharatanatyam which are a combination of footwork and hand gestures. 
Learning 4 sets of Adavus with sorkattu
Introduction to the concept of Laya (speed) and practice the adavus to 3 speeds. 

Beginner Level 2

approx 6 months

Adavus

Learning next 4 sets of adavu with sorkattu and practice adavus to 3 speeds 

Hastas and Bedas

Learning of double hand posture "Samyuktha Hasta"
Learning of eye movements "Drishti Bhedam"
Learning of neck movements "Greeva Bhedam"
Learning of chest movements "Uraha Bhedam"

Anga Sudham

Learning the use of eyes, head, and neck in coordination with the body movements.
Learning on how they can add grace to the body

Intermidiate Level 1

approx 6 months

Adavus

Learning next 2 sets of adavu with sorkattu and practice the adavus in 3 speeds

Alarippu

learning the first piece of bharathanatyam "Alarippu" with tala, raga and sorkattu and practice the sorkattu with tala

bhedas

Learning of stomach movements "Uthara bhedam"

Intermidiate Level 2

approx 6 months

Kowthuvam

learning the item Kowthuvam with raga and sorkattu and practice the sorkattu with Tala

Jathishwaram

learning the piece of bharathanatyam "Jathishwaram" with tala, raga and learn the jathi to recite with Tala

Bhedas

Learning of hip movements"Gati bhedam"

Advance Level 1

approx 6 months

Varnam

learning the lengthiest piece of bharatanatyam "Varnam" with Tala, Raga and learn the jathis to recite with thala.
learning the meaning of the complete songs.
lerning the expression part "bhava"

Charis according to Natya Sastra

learning of Bhoomi-charis which is the combination of hand legs and feets on floor

Bhedas

Advanced Level 2

approx 6 months

Padam

Learning next 2 sets of adavu with sorkattu and practice the adavus in 3 speeds

Thillana

learning the first piece of bharathanatyam "Alarippu" with tala, raga and sorkattu and practice the sorkattu with tala
