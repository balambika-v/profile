FAQ

How many years does it take to do Salangai poojai and Arangetram?

It depends on individual capacity.
For Salangai poojai the deciding criteria are finishing intermediate level with perfection and good rhythmic knowledge.
For Arangetram the deciding criteria are finishing advanced level and maturity in understanding and conveying the concept through the dance.
To know more about it please refer our course details.

Is the classes conducted 1-on-1 or in groups?

Classes will be conducted in pairs of 2 to maximum 4 where students gets company to learn together with needed attention to everyone, classes will be 1 hour each.

What are the timings for the class?

Timings are based on the availability of slots and your location. To know the available time slot that suits you please contact us.

Is there weekend classes?

Classes are not conducted over the weekend. 

Is offline classes conducted?

Offline regular classes are conducted only in case of Salangai poojai and Arangetram.
In case of Salangai poojai few days of offline class and for Arangetram minimum 1 month of offline class is suggested.

Can you choreograph for a song?

Till advanced level it is difficult to capture the essence and deliver the concept to the audience. So song choreography will be taught to students from advanced level.
